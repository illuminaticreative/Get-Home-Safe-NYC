Get Home Safe NYC

In the wake of Hurricane Sandy, many New Yorkers have been forced to evacuate their homes, unsure when they'll be able to return. The purpose of this site is to offer residents a way to share information about specific buildings, letting others know what to expect at their apartment and if it's safe to go home.

If you know the status of a building please update your neighbors here.
